Welcome to Hospital Management System

A Hospital Management System (HMS) is an integrated information system for managing all aspects of a hospital's operations such as medical, administrative, legal, and compliance. It includes electronic health records,and business intelligence. It helps in patient management, maintaing electronic medical records (EMR), and Appointment scheduling.
